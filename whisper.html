PROJECT: WHISPER KEY INFORMATION

Project Arrival Date: April 11th, 2020
Image

On the Eleventh of April, 2020, an unknown specimen arrived at Veles Laboratory. This specimen was given the key-name: Whisper.

It was determined that Whisper is made from a material very similar to human muscle and nerves. It was also determined that Whisper was able to react to painful stimuli. Sentience was NOT determined.

Noah Cameron currently remains as Head Researcher regarding all experiments and research conducted on Whisper.


LOGS

Image
April 11th, 2020

Whisper arrival at Veles Laboratory and given the key-name 'Whisper'. Hana Stone was assigned as Head Researcher.

April 31st, 2020

Key Information regarding Whisper updated. It was determined Whisper was made from a material very similar to human muscle and nerves. It was determined that Whisper was able to react to painful stimuli. Whisper's potential sentience was not determined. Vixen Ruiz was recorded as stating that research on Whisper's ability to impact human behaviour should be 'looked into'.

May 8th, 2020

Hana Stone requested to be removed from all research and studies conducted on Whisper. Stating 'it's like it's in my mind'. Robert Brown approved this request, and Noah Cameron was made new Head Researcher.

May 15th, 2020

Ash Valencia puts in an offical request to end all research and studies conducted on Whisper, stating a potential human risk if the project remained in less-secured containment. Noah Cameron denied this request.

July 4th, 2020

An accident occurs within an experiment conducted on Whisper which resulted in near-injury for Vixen Ruiz.

August 22nd, 2020

Noah Cameron determined the next best step regarding Whisper was to transport samples to different laboratories across the country. Ash Valencia was recorded as making their disagreement clear.

August 27th, 2020

Jasper Mueller began keeping a record of logs of the locations Whisper samples were being transported to.
