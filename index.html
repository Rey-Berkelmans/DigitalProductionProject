IMPORTANT NEWS


Veles Laboratory Shuts Down

September 20th, 2020

After a tragic fire that destroyed the majority of Veles Laboratory and ended the life of several staff members on the twelfth of September, the decision was made to shut down the Laboratory after four long years of research and innovation.

Noah Cameron has stated that he still remains avaliable to reachout to regarding any of Veles Laboratory previous projects.


Research Expanded

August 23rd, 2020

Veles Laboratory has begun to expand and share research across the country to many different laboratories and research facilities. This has involved the shipment of samples and research undertaken on several different projects.

Noah Cameron has taken lead on this expansion, and has stated that he hopes that the studies conducted within Veles Laboratory can help humanity as a whole.


Notice Regarding Ex-Employee

July 18th, 2020

On the ninth of July, Hana Stone was let go from Veles Laboratory due to behavioural issues and harassment towards other staff. Since then, Hana Stone has been spreading inappropriate and false rumours regarding the research conducted within Veles Laboratory.

Veles Laboratory is making a statement to assure that all these rumours are the lies of a upset ex-employee, and have no bearing in reality.
